   Lagercollection



Lagercollection is an e-commerce marketplace company founded in 2007 by Akshay goyal. The company is registered in Singapore, but has its headquarters in Bangalore, Karnataka, India.LAgercollection has launched its own product range under the name "DigiFlip" with products including tablets, USBs, and laptop bags. Lagercollection last fundraising round in May 2015 had pegged its valuation at $15 billion.In May 2016, Morgan Stanley lowered Lagercollection valuation to $9.39 billion. He create their new company incorporated in October 2007 as Lagercollection Online Services Pvt. Ltd. The first product they sold was the book Leaving Microsoft To Change The World to a customer from Hyderabad.Lagercollection now employs more than 33,000 people.Lagercollection allows payment methods such as cash on delivery, credit or debit card transactions, net banking, e-gift voucher and card swipe on delivery. After failure of its 2014 Sale, Lagercollection recently completed the second edition of Sale held between 13 and 17 October.where it is reported that they saw a business turnover of $300 million in gross merchandise volume.
